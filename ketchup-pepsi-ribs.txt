Ingredients
 - Country style ribs
 - Ketchup
 - Pepsi
 - Adobo seasoning

Directions:
 1. Season ribs with Adobo
 2. Mix ketchup and pepsi in a bowl to a nice BBQ saucy consistency.
 3. Coat Ribs with ketchup pepsi sauce
 4. Preheat oven to 375
 5. Cook for an hour or until ribs all done.  Turn over half way through.
